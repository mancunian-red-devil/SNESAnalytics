Each group member is represented by a circle. The size of the circle denotes the number of messages sent by the member. The link between any two members represents a measure of how often the two members send messages around each other. So if two members often send messages close to each other, then it's likely that they are often involved in the same conversation. Thus invariably, more active members will be around the centre while dormant members are, literally, on the fringes! Double click on a circle to look at links for the corresponding group member.
